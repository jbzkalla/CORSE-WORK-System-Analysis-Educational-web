"A QUALITY FOUNDED INSTITUTION IN THE HEART OF UGANDA".

The institution was founded in 1997 by the Dr.professor
Kato Joseph Bwanika. It was the best institution ever known in the heart
of Uganda known for the best employees produced between 1999-2010. Its original name was
"Twins royal institute" but later it changed to the current name as a clue to the devised
changes for administrative modification. Today, it is still considered among the top 5 great Ugandan institutions.
By 2012, it had contributed around 700 employees in the job market and as per now many more are being produced.
The institution has gone under a re-innovation process since most of the infrastructures are aged.
And as we talk now, Kalla International University has one of the best infrastuctures around town
and this is the Charles Mukasa Library.
